2. How to win?

The player wins the game if they are able to get five of their pieces in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) before their timer runs out.


3. Interesting historical origin

According to legend, Gobang originated in the period of Emperor Yao more than 4,000 years ago. It has a longer history than Go. It may have been before Yao created Go. It's exactly the same as Go. There are also legends that Gobang was originally popular in ethnic minority areas, and then gradually evolved into Go and spread throughout the descendants of Yan and Huang.
